My favorite, and the most underated sport, Badminton


Badminton was introduced to me early on as a child, and is a fun sport that is not talked about enough. Despite not being big into sports, I found myself having a lot of fun whenever I would play badminton with my father, especially due to the fast paced nature of the game. I understand that some people think it may be boring to watch, but when you acctually play it, you have so much fun. However, this sport doesn't get enough recognition, possibly for it's simplicity. This page cointains the rules for the sport so if you consider playing it and have the materials:

  • A really big net
  • Two plus badminton rackets
  • A shuttlecock
Then you can play the game! I prefer to play outside because of the natural breathtaking experience. However, profesionals play it inside because they don't want the weather to interfere with the gameplay. One more little detail, I believe that Badminton orignated a European sport but it was quickly adopted by many of all of Asia including but not limited to: China, India, Japan, Malaysia, South Korea, Pakistan, etc.
